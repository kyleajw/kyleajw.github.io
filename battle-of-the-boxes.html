Battle of the Boxes
About
‘Battle of the Boxes’ is my first submitted group project in the second year of the course. In this project, I was assigned as project lead along with lead programmer among our group of three.

‘Battle of the Boxes’ is an FPS game based within varying packaging warehouses. The main weapon in the environment are throwable boxes.
